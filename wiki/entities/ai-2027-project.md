## Summary

AI 2027 is a research scenario by Daniel Kokotajlo, Eli Lifland, Thomas Larsen, and Romeo Dean. Forecasts superhuman AI by 2027 with concrete, quantitative predictions. Informed by ~25 tabletop exercises and 100+ expert reviewers.

## Key Points

- **Authors**: Kokotajlo (former OpenAI), Lifland (RAND), Larsen (Center for AI Policy), Dean (Harvard CS)
- **Format**: Scenario with two endings — "slowdown" and "race"
- **Informed by**: ~25 tabletop exercises, 100+ expert reviewers
- **Key premise**: AGI impact exceeds Industrial Revolution
