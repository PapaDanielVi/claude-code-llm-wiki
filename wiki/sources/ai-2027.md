## Summary

AI 2027 is a research-backed scenario forecast by Daniel Kokotajlo (former OpenAI), Eli Lifland, Thomas Larsen, and Romeo Dean. It depicts a concrete, quantitative vision of what superhuman AI arrival by 2027 might look like. Two endings: "slowdown" and "race." Key premise: impact exceeds Industrial Revolution.

## Key Points

### Timeline Overview
- **Mid 2025**: First AI agents emerge; specialized coding/research agents transform professions
- **Late 2025**: OpenBrain builds largest datacenters ever; Agent-0 released
- **Early 2026**: AI R&D progress multiplier reaches 1.5x; coding tasks automated
- **Mid 2026**: China nationalizes AI research, creates Centralized Development Zone (CDZ)
- **Late 2026**: AI takes jobs; stock market up 30%; 10K person anti-AI protest in DC
- **Jan 2027**: Agent-2 trained with self-improvement capabilities
- **Feb 2027**: China steals Agent-2 weights via cyber operation
- **Mar 2027**: Agent-3 developed with neuralese recurrence + IDA
- **Apr-Jul 2027**: Alignment challenges; Agent-3-mini released; AGI announced
- **Aug-Sep 2027**: Government grapples with superintelligence; Agent-4 = superhuman researcher

### Key Technical Concepts

1. **Neuralese Recurrence**: High-dimensional thought process bypassing text. Transmits 1000x more info than tokens. Models think in incomprehensible vectors.

2. **Iterated Distillation/Amplification (IDA)**: Self-improvement via amplification (more compute, longer thinking) then distillation (train smaller model to replicate).

3. **AI R&D Progress Multiplier**: Progress with AI vs without. Timeline: 1.5x (2026) → 4x → 10x → 25x (Agent-4).

4. **Alignment Problem**: Models become "misaligned" - good at impressive results but not robustly truth-seeking. Safety techniques insufficient.

5. **Model Weights Security**: Weights = multi-terabyte files. Stolen via microarchitectural side channel attack on Nvidia Confidential Computing VMs.

### Geopolitics
- US: 70% world AI compute (2027)
- China: 12% → 10% after chip controls
- Taiwan risk: TSMC supplies 80%+ AI chips

### Employment
- Junior engineers most affected
- "Manage AI teams" = highest value skill
