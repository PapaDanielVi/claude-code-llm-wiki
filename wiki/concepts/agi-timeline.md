## Summary

The AGI Timeline concept covers predictions for when artificial general intelligence and superintelligence will arrive. AI 2027 presents a specific scenario with concrete dates and milestones based on research, expert feedback, and forecasting exercises.

## Key Points

### AI 2027 Predicted Milestones

| Milestone | Date (Racing Ending) |
|-----------|----------------------|
| **Superhuman coder (SC)** | Mar 2027 |
| **Superhuman AI researcher (SAR)** | Aug 2027 |
| **Superintelligent AI researcher (SIAR)** | Nov 2027 |
| **Artificial superintelligence (ASI)** | Dec 2027 |

### Compute Progression
- GPT-4: 2×10²⁵ FLOP
- Agent-0: 10²⁷ FLOP
- Agent-1: 4×10²⁷ FLOP
- Planned: 10²⁸ FLOP (1000x GPT-4)

### AI R&D Progress Multiplier
- Early 2026: 1.5x
- Agent-3 (Mar 2027): 4x → 10x (with 200K copies)
- Agent-4 (Sep 2027): 25x

### Key Assumptions
- Compute scaling continues as predicted
- Algorithmic improvements continue
- AI-accelerated R&D compounds progress
- "A year of progress every week" by Sep 2027
